About Us

Established in 1991, N.G. Bhoir Construction has been a trusted name in the civil and construction industry. Led by Mr. Narayan Gajanan Bhoir, we specialize in civil, earthwork, and fabrication projects for government, semi-government, and private clients. Our commitment to safety, quality, and timely delivery has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Services

Civil Construction

With over 30 years of experience, we provide comprehensive civil construction services. Our expertise covers residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We have successfully completed projects for major clients like **JNPT** and **ITD Cementation**.

Earthwork Services

Our earthwork services include excavation, grading, and site preparation. We specialize in land reclamation and ground improvement, with projects such as the **JNPT coastal berth reclamation** and **ground improvement for Navi Mumbai SEZ**.

Fabrication Services

We offer steel fabrication services for industrial structures, ensuring precise installation and durability. Our work includes the fabrication of the **VTMS steel tower** at **JNPT**.

Project Highlights

Land Reclamation at JNPT

This ₹11 crore project involved the reclamation of land for the development of a coastal berth at JNPT. We successfully handled the excavation and site development with precision and adherence to environmental standards.

Ground Improvement for Navi Mumbai SEZ

For this ₹2.66 crore project, we enhanced the stability of the land to prepare it for future development. This work involved geotextile installation and rock filling, ensuring long-term structural integrity.

Steel Fabrication for VTMS Tower at JNPT

We fabricated and erected the **Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) steel tower** at JNPT, valued at ₹50 lakh. This project required precision and compliance with port safety standards.
