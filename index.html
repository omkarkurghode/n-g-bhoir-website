About M/s. N.G. Bhoir

Established in 1991, M/s. N.G. Bhoir is a leading civil engineering company with expertise in civil work, earthwork, and construction projects. For over three decades, we have delivered quality solutions while maintaining high safety standards in every project we undertake.

Our mission is to provide top-notch civil services while ensuring timely project delivery and unparalleled customer satisfaction. We work across government, semi-government, and private sectors, contributing to large-scale infrastructure development.

Core Values: Safety, Quality, Reliability, and Customer Satisfaction.
